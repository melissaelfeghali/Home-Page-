About Us

TuneTrip is a sound design studio that combines sound and image into an immersive experience. In addition, to our constatntly evolving library of sounds and animations, this studio crafts original sounds experiences for multiple purposes such as films, animations, interactive projects, etc.

Our objective is to showcase the power of sound in stimulating our minds through accessing different memories, emotions, situations, imageries and even locations.

Every week, we post a track that would be translated into an animation, design or art piece. Tell us what you are hearing and help us recreate your trip!
